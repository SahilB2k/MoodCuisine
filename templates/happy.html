MAIN COURSES:

● DAL MAKHANI ●

Indulge in the richness of Dal Makhani, a classic North Indian dish featuring black lentils and kidney beans simmered in a luscious tomato-based gravy.

● PANEER FRY ●

Delight in the savory goodness of this classic Indian dish. Tender cubes of paneer, infused with a blend of aromatic spices, are gently pan-fried to golden perfection.

● CHICKEN TIKKA MASALA ●

Tender pieces of grilled chicken are smothered in a creamy tomato-based sauce infused with aromatic spices. With each bite, savor the perfect balance of heat and creaminess.

● PASTA PRIMAVERA ●

Experience the vibrant flavors of this classic Italian dish.Colorful vegetables, such as bell peppers, cherry tomatoes, and broccoli, are sautéed to perfection and tossed with al dente pasta.

● CAPRESE CHICKEN ●

Revel in the harmony of flavors in this mouthwatering dish. Tender chicken pieces are adorned with ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil leaves, creating a vibrant and delicious combination.p>

● EGGPLANT PARMESAN ●

Dive into an Italian classic. Tender eggplant slices, coated in crispy breadcrumbs, are baked to perfection with marinara sauce and topped with gooey melted cheese.

● ZUCCHINI NOODLES ●

Fresh zucchini is spiralized into noodles and sautéed until tender, then tossed seasoning for a low-carb and gluten-free alternative.

DESSERTS:

● SHRIKHAND ●

Indulge in the creamy sweetness of Shrikhand, a dessert made with strained yogurt. This delightful treat features smooth, thick yogurt that's been hung to remove excess whey, resulting in a rich and concentrated base.

● KULFI ●

Indulge in the rich and creamy flavors of this traditional Indian dessert. Made from thickened milk and variety of aromatic spices such as cardamom and saffron, Kulfi offers a luxurious and satisfying treat.

● MINT SMOOTHIE ●

Refresh your senses with this invigorating blend. Fresh mint leaves are combined with creamy yogurt, tangy lime juice, and a touch of honey for a cool and revitalizing drink.

● STRAWBERRY PARFAIT ●

Dive into layers of fruity goodness with this delightful treat. Fresh strawberries are layered with creamy yogurt and crunchy granola,creating perfect balance of sweetness and texture.

● CHOCOLATE AVACADO ●

Delight in a guilt-free dessert experience with this creamy delight. Smooth avocado blends seamlessly with rich cocoa powder, offering a decadent mousse that satisfies your sweet tooth without the guilt.

● CHEESE CAKE ●

Enjoy a classic dessert favorite. A smooth and creamy filling rests atop a buttery graham cracker crust, creating a decadent treat that melts in your mouth with every bite. Topped with a luscious fruit compote or a drizzle of caramel sauce, it's the perfect dessert for any occasion.

● LEMON PIE ●

Savor the tangy sweetness of this classic dessert. A buttery, flaky crust holds a zesty lemon filling, bursting with citrus flavor. Each bite offers a perfect balance of tartness and sweetness, making it a refreshing and delightful treat for any occasion.

● MACARONS ●

Delight in these exquisite French treats. Crisp meringue shells sandwich a creamy ganache or fruity filling, creating a symphony of textures and flavors. With each bite-sized indulgence, experience the perfect balance of sweetness and elegance, making them a delightful addition to any dessert spread.
